ABOUT AJAY-ATUL

Ajay and Atul Gogavale were born to Ashok Gogavale, a Revenue Department officer in Alandi in Pune. Both were born in Pune and brought up in several villages in western Maharashtra, including Junnar, Shirur, Manchar and Ghodegaon as their father had a transferable job. Atul was born on 11 September 1974 and Ajay his younger brother born on 21 August 1976.In their childhood, they were not much attracted towards academics. But their interest in music developed while they were still in school. Around this time, they started experimenting with music. In an NCC competition, Ajay played an existing composition differently,and they won the prize for their experiment.jay & Atul Gogavale are an embodiment of simplicity and purity which is reflected in each of their musical compositions that narrate the innermost human emotions like no other. All this, with no formal musical background. Renowned as one of India’s leading music directors, the illustrious duo has earned global recognition for creating a niche in the musical arena with exceptional music compositions and tracks.Starting their careers with commercial jingles, ballets and advertisements, the duo went on to compose for plays, private albums, and movies. Vishwavinayak, a spiritual non-filmy music album with Ganesha Symphonic Chants. The album topped international charts and proved to be a big break for the duo, paving their way into the Indian music industry.

They did not have a musical background. Although they were not supported by their family directly for music,they were never denied any assistance. As their family could not afford the instruments for their musical endeavours, they started their musical ventures through their school, temples, local bands, etc. Despite not getting direct training, they learned a lot from these ventures.n Marathi cinema, Mauli, Jaundya Na Balasaheb, Lai Bhari, Fandry, Natrang, Jatra, Saavarkhed - Ek Gaon, Aga Bai Areccha, Jabardast , Uladhaal and musical blockbuster as Natrang Jogwa & Sairat are some of the Marathi movies which gained massive attention from the masses for their exceptional music tracks.Some of their most popular compositions from Marathi include Zingaat, Wajle Ki Bara, Apsara Aali, Kombdi Palali, Malhar Wari, Morya, Lallati Bhandar, Mauli, Bring It On, and Dolby Song, to name a few.Ajay & Atul have been bestowed with the National Award for Best Music Direction for 'Jogwa' at the 56th National Awards. They are the only music directors to have been conferred upon the honour for a Marathi movie. The duo has won 29 accolades for music direction with Ajay winning more than nine awards for Best Male Playback Singer.The duo’s popularity has been internationally recognised when they made their debut on the 2015 Forbes India Celebrity 100 List, securing a position at 82. They are also the first Indian music directors to record at Sony Scoring Studios in Hollywood for Marathi movie Sairat. ‘Zingaat’ from the same movie turned out to be a huge hit and was the first Marathi song to be recognised worldwide.
